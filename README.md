# 🌍 CarbonLens AI

> Measure Today. Improve Tomorrow. Build a Sustainable Future.

## 📖 Introduction

CarbonLens AI is an AI-powered carbon footprint awareness platform designed to help individuals calculate, understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact through personalized insights, sustainability scoring, smart recommendations, achievement systems, and interactive analytics.

Many people want to live more sustainably but often struggle to understand how their everyday choices contribute to carbon emissions. Existing carbon calculators typically provide a single result and leave users without clear guidance on what to do next. CarbonLens AI addresses this challenge by transforming environmental data into actionable insights and creating a continuous sustainability journey rather than a one-time calculation.

The platform combines carbon footprint analysis, intelligent recommendations, progress tracking, gamification, and visual analytics to make sustainability measurable, engaging, and easy to understand for everyone.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the most significant global challenges of our time. Although awareness about environmental sustainability is increasing, many individuals lack access to personalized tools that clearly explain their environmental impact and guide them toward meaningful improvements.

Traditional carbon calculators often:
- Provide limited feedback
- Lack personalization
- Do not track long-term progress
- Fail to motivate users to maintain sustainable habits
- Offer generic recommendations that may not apply to individual lifestyles

CarbonLens AI was created to bridge this gap by providing a comprehensive platform that not only calculates carbon emissions but also helps users understand, improve, and sustain environmentally responsible behaviors.

## 💡 How It Works

Users provide information about:

- Energy consumption

Historical data is stored so users can monitor improvements and measure long-term sustainability growth.
